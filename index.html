Who Am I?

Hello! My story isn't a straight line, but an exciting journey fuelled by curiosity, perseverance and the determination to push my own boundaries.

My adventure began not in a lecture hall, but on the workshop floor with an apprenticeship as an Automation Technician. For several years, I was immersed in managing projects, assembling electrical systems, put them into operation and finally switched into hardware planning as a Junior Project Manager. That practical experience fueled my hunger for deeper knowledge.

Driven by this ambition, I completed an HF degree in electrical engineering. But this degree was not enough. I was looking for a bigger challenge and found what I was looking for: ETH Zurich. Lacking the traditional academic Matura, I discovered an alternative route, the comprehensive entrance exam. With focused discipline and the power of self-driven learning via the internet, I didn't just prepare – I succeeded.

Walking onto the ETH campus felt like unlocking a new level. I earned my Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering, specializing in robotics, systems and control, machine learning, and computer vision. Now I'm finishing my Master's degree, diving deep into advanced machine learning, probabilistic AI, and agile product development (graduating August 2025). After years of theory, I'm eager to get back to building and solving real problems.

This unconventional path reflects how I approach challenges: with discipline and tireless commitment. To see how this translates into tangible results, I invite you to explore the personal and academic projects in my Projects section. For a detailed breakdown of my professional roles and academic qualifications, please see the Experience and Education sections, respectively.
