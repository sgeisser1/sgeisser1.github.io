MECFcam

Low-latency video stream connection for a model railroad simulator.

The MECFcam is a project to create a low-latency video stream connection for a model railroad simulator. We have an H0 model railroad layout with a real driver's cab of the MECF. So far the camera has been operated with an analog camera and we want a digital system that will provide a higher video resolution.
